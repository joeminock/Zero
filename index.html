How Startbox Works

Whether you're handy or not, we've made it super fun and easy to take any project from start to finish

Step 1. Select a Project

Startbox video lessons and how-to projects cover everything from Pinterest® inspired craft projects to home improvement & honey do's. Click "Get Started!" on any project and you'll be celebrating success in no time!

Step 2. Watch Video, Be Handy!

Each project comes with a comprehensive list of tools and necessary supplies. Once you're ready to go, each project how-to video is broken down into short, easy to understand video segments that you can watch as many times as you want!

Step 3. Smile, You Did It!

There's nothing more fulfilling than doing something you've never done before. Startbox will help you Get Started and our easy to follow projects and video tasks make sure you finish with a smile everytime!
